How SkillSync
actually works

A three-step AI workflow that turns scattered talent data into precision matches.

01

Ingest & Map Skills

Connect your HRIS, LinkedIn, or upload manually. Our AI parses resumes, project histories, and feedback to build verified skill profiles automatically.

02

Define Project Requirements

Describe a project or role in plain language. SkillSync decomposes it into weighted skill vectors using NLP and your org's historical data.

03

Receive Ranked Matches

Get a ranked shortlist with explainable AI scores, availability windows, and growth trajectory data — not just a keyword match.

04

Close Gaps Proactively

SkillSync flags future bottlenecks before they happen and recommends training, hiring, or restructuring — all with ROI projections.

Our story

Built by people who've
felt the pain firsthand

SkillSync AI was born in 2023 inside a 400-person product studio that kept mis-staffing projects. The right engineers were always on the wrong teams. Talent was invisible. Decisions were made on gut feel, spreadsheets, and hallway conversations.

Our founding team — engineering leads, an ML researcher, and a people-ops veteran — spent six months building what they wished existed: an AI system that actually understands skills, not just job titles.

Today SkillSync powers talent decisions at companies from 10 to 10,000 employees. We're obsessed with one thing: making sure the right person is always in the right place at the right time.
